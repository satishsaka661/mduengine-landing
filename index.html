What MDU Engine does

  • Validates whether your data window is decision-ready (7–30 days recommended).
  • Normalizes exports into an engine-ready daily schema.
  • Simulates outcomes with downside-risk protection.
  • Explains every recommendation and blocks unsafe actions.
  • Logs an audit snapshot (versions + settings + decision summary).
MDU Engine provides decision support based on uploaded data. It does not execute changes automatically and should not be considered financial or investment advice.

From “gut feel” to defensible decisions.

Most ad accounts fail from two extremes: reacting too fast to noise, or waiting too long while losses compound. MDU Engine is designed to be conservative, explainable, and operationally usable.

Decision Scenarios

Defined situations like low data window, tracking instability, volatile spend spikes, or negative net-value drift — each produces a consistent action with rationale.

Loss Protection Rules

Hard safety rails that block risky recommendations when downside risk exceeds your selected threshold. Defaults to cautious behavior.

Health Score

A single quality indicator showing whether your signal is stable enough to act on today. Improves with clean daily data.

Explainable outputs

No black-box “increase budget” advice. Every decision includes what happened, what could go wrong, and what to do next.
